What is Pizza

Pizza is a baked, flatbread typically topped with tomato sauce and cheese, often with various meats and vegetables. Originating in Italy, it's a globally popular and versatile dish.

Pizza's History

Pizza evolved from ancient flatbreads, but modern pizza began in 18th/19th century Naples as a cheap food. Italian immigrants popularized it in the U.S., leading to its global spread.
